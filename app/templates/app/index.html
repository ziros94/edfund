What is EdFund?

EdFund is a platform for adults to contribute to school clubs of their choice. Whether you're an alumni or someone with no affiliation to the school, you can help make a difference for students who attend these extracurriculars! Your donation will help these clubs provide better opportunities and experiences for their students!
